# Reviewer Overview

## Your Role

As a **Reviewer**, you ensure submission quality by evaluating whether submissions meet project standards. Your feedback shapes the quality of the training data and helps submitters improve.

## Key Responsibilities

- 🔍 **Evaluate Submissions** - Assess completeness, clarity, and alignment with guidelines
- 💬 **Provide Constructive Feedback** - Explain issues clearly and suggest improvements
- ✅ **Accept or Reject** - Make clear decisions based on established criteria
- 📊 **Maintain Consistency** - Apply rubrics uniformly across all submissions

## What You're Evaluating

1. **Intent Quality** - Is it clear, measurable, and appropriately scoped?
2. **System Prompt** - Does it enable the intent without contradictions?
3. **User Prompts** - Are they realistic and well-contextualized?
4. **Bad Response** - Does it demonstrate a clear, realistic failure mode?
5. **Good Response** - Does it meet the intent and avoid the failure?

## Decision Framework

:::tip Your Decision Should Be Based On:
- Does this submission meet the quality bar?
- Would this be useful for training an AI model?
- Are all required components present and correct?
- Is the failure mode clear and realistic?
:::

## Common Review Scenarios

- ✅ **Accept** - All criteria met, minor or no issues
- ❌ **Reject with Feedback** - Fixable issues that need submitter revision
- 🤔 **Edge Case** - Escalate to adjudicator if unclear

## Impact of Your Work

Your reviews directly impact:
- Training data quality
- Submitter learning and improvement
- Overall project success
- AI model performance
