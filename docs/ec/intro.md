# Welcome to Supernaut EC Guidelines

This documentation site is your **single source of truth** for all project guidelines, whether you're an External Contributor (Submitter, Reviewer, Adjudicator) or part of the internal Snorkel/Mistral team.

## Quick Start by Role

Choose your role to jump directly to the most relevant information:

- **[Submitter](/roles/submitter/overview)** - Create high-quality submissions
- **[Reviewer](/roles/reviewer/overview)** - Provide consistent, constructive feedback  
- **[Adjudicator](/roles/adjudicator/overview)** - Resolve complex edge cases

## How to Use This Site

### Two Ways to Navigate

1. **By Role** - Start with your role's overview, checklist, and common pitfalls
2. **By Reference** - Deep dive into workflow steps, failure modes, tools, and system prompts

### What You'll Find Here

- ✅ **Practical Checklists** - Step-by-step guides for your role
- 📖 **Detailed Reference** - In-depth explanations of workflows and concepts
- 🔧 **Tool Documentation** - How to use web search, news search, and other tools
- ❓ **FAQ & Glossary** - Quick answers and term definitions
- 📝 **Changelog** - Track updates and changes to guidelines

## Important Notes

- All pages show an **Effective Date** and **Owners** at the top
- Use the **search bar** to quickly find specific topics
- Check the **[Changelog](/reference/changelog)** regularly for updates
- Each heading has a **deep-link anchor** for easy sharing

## Need Help?

- Check the [FAQ & Glossary](/reference/faq-glossary) first
- Review the [Common Mistakes](/roles/submitter/common-mistakes) pages for your role
- Look at the [Failure Modes](/reference/failure-modes/overview) reference
